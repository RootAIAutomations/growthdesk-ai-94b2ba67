The Problem

Solo service providers are drowning in admin

"I juggle WhatsApp, Gmail, Notion, a spreadsheet, and an Instagram scheduler — just to run my freelance business."

😰 Before GrowthDesk

  • Client notes scattered across 5 apps
  • Writing outreach messages from scratch every time
  • Forgetting to follow up with warm leads
  • No time to plan social content consistently

✅ After GrowthDesk

  • One dashboard for all clients + history
  • AI generates personalised outreach in seconds
  • Automatic follow-up reminders
  • AI-written 7-day content calendar
The Solution

Everything a solo provider needs — in one AI hub

👥

Client CRM

Add clients with tags, status, notes, last contact date. Track everything in one place.

✍️

AI Outreach Drafts

One click generates personalised WhatsApp, Email & LinkedIn drafts using client context.

📅

Content Calendar

AI generates a 7-day plan — Instagram captions, LinkedIn posts, blog openers.

⏰

Follow-Up Reminders

Dashboard flags clients not contacted in N days. Never let a warm lead go cold.

💬

Conversation Log

Log every interaction by channel. Voice mic fills notes hands-free via Web Speech API.

📚

Content Library

Save your best posts, reuse them. Search and filter by platform or tag.
